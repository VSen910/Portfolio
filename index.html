About me

My name is Vineet Sen, I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Dharwad. I'm currently in my 8th semester and will be graduating in Apr 2025.

I've been a gamer all my life, and interestingly, it was gaming that got me into programming too. In my 6th grade, I wrote my first ever program, a Mario-like game in Java, which I built following an online tutorial (spoiler alert: barely understood 10-20% of the code I wrote). This was the project that made me realize the satisfation of creating a program from scratch and seeing it work.

I eventually decided I wanted to pursue a career in software after that, and started learning Java right away. I then moved on to learning more languages including C and C++ along with basic Android Development with Java before joining college.

In college, I started from learning Android Development with Java, then learnt Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and eventually Flutter, which I have stuck with ever since. I also learnt Backend Development with Node.js, Express and MongoDB, and have worked on a few projects with these technologies.

I was also a part of the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at my college where I and my team conducted the Compose Camp and the Flutter Forward Extended where I taught 150+ students about Kotlin and basic layout in Flutter.
